# Code of Conduct

This project adopts a simple standard: be respectful, constructive, and easy to collaborate with.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional
- Give actionable feedback
- Assume good intent unless there is a clear reason not to
- Keep discussions focused on improving the work
- Welcome contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks
- Discrimination or exclusionary behavior
- Trolling, intimidation, or hostility
- Publishing private information without consent
- Repeated bad-faith interaction

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

If something crosses the line, open a private report through the security/contact path used by the maintainer, or reach out directly to the maintainer if appropriate.
